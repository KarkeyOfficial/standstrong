The Impact of Bullying

Bullying can have significant and lasting effects on the well-being of those who experience it.

Physical Impact

  • Injuries
  • Sleep problems
  • Stomach aches
  • Headaches

Mental Impact

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Increased feelings of sadness and loneliness
  • Loss of self-confidence
  • Increased self-criticism
  • Suicidal thoughts

Emotional Impact

  • Feeling unsafe and afraid
  • Feeling confused and stressed out
  • Feeling ashamed, angry, guilty, or hopeless
  • Low self-esteem

Social Impact

  • Becoming isolated or withdrawn
  • Losing interest in normal activities
  • Difficulty in forming and maintaining relationships
  • Feeling disconnected from school

Spiritual Impact

  • Feeling a violation of personal rights and dignity
