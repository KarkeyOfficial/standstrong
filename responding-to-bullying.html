Responding to Bullying

If you are experiencing bullying, it's important to remember that you are not alone and there are things you can do.

Strategies for Victims

  1. Talk to someone you trust: Find a friend, family member, teacher, counselor, or another adult you feel comfortable talking to. Sharing your experience can help you feel less alone and get support. They can offer advice and help you figure out what to do next.
  2. Seek help from an agency or support service: There are many organizations dedicated to helping young people who are being bullied. These resources can provide guidance, support, and strategies for dealing with bullying. (See our Helpful Resources page for links).

Remember, it's not your fault, and you deserve to feel safe and respected.
