Preventing Bullying at OPAC

Creating a bully-free environment requires a community effort. Here are some strategies to help prevent bullying in our school:

Prevention Strategies

  1. Implement and enforce clear anti-bullying policies: OPAC should have well-defined rules against bullying with clear consequences for those who engage in it. These policies should be consistently communicated to all students, staff, and parents and enforced fairly.
  2. Promote a positive school climate: Foster a culture of respect, empathy, and inclusion within OPAC. Encourage kindness, understanding, and celebrating diversity. This can be done through school-wide initiatives, classroom activities, and leading by example.

By working together, we can make OPAC a place where everyone feels safe and respected.
